FAQ

At first you have to fill the application form via the website in your best capacity explaining about your relevant experience. Based upon the application and other details we will shortlist the members for a 1 on 1 screening to learn more about you.

After the selection based upon the application form. Our mentors will have a 1 on 1 interview screening round to learn more about your web2 and web3 domain related knowledge. We'll make a decision based upon your skills, past experience, willingness and other parameters to further shortlist and finalize your application selection part after this.

It will be a 6 weeks program starting from mid of January. It will take around 30-40 hours of your time per week. It's a self paced learning program. Being a student or a full-time employee is not a barrier. You can even complete it early as per your bandwidth, so that you can work more on the mentioned topics on a weekly basis.

As this is the first Internship Program from our end, we will be shortlisting only 50 members as part of the program, who will be assigned to mentors. As there will be less number of participants so more engagement and focus will be put forward by our mentors and team members so as to help you effectively learn more on the mentioned topics.

The program is absolutely FREE of cost.

This program has been curated for individuals who want to learn about the basics of web3 and Tezos Blockchain. Being a student or a full-time employee is not a barrier.
